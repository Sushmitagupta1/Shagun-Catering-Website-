Our Story

Serving joy to people since 1989 — the legacy of Upper Crust & Shagun Catering.

Since 1989

A Legacy of
Culinary Passion

Shagun Catering was founded in 2014 by Lester D'souza with a singular vision: to bring the excellence of Upper Crust's legendary delicacies into the world of catering. What started as a dream has quickly evolved into a fine boutique catering service with a strong, dedicated team based in Ahmedabad.

At Shagun, we understand that food is deeply intertwined with memories. That's why we go the extra mile for every occasion. With over 32 years of culinary heritage backing us, we cater to both grand celebrations and intimate gatherings — from corporate conferences to weddings, sangeet ceremonies, and every special moment in between.

Our menus span exotic soups and starters, mocktails, Indian classics (Gujarati, Punjabi, Rajasthani), and international cuisines including Chinese, Italian, Mediterranean, Mexican, and Thai — all finished with a variety of exquisite desserts.

Founders

The People Behind
the Flavours

A husband-wife duo whose passion for food and hospitality built a culinary legacy in Ahmedabad.

LD

Lester D'souza

Founder

Lester's passion for food drew him to the Institute of Hotel Management, followed by training at the Taj Mansingh in Delhi. He returned to Ahmedabad and created Upper Crust — introducing the city to an unparalleled bakery and culinary experience. After establishing the brand, he launched Shagun Catering, which is now a household name across weddings, receptions, sangeet, corporate events, and more.

MD

Monisha D'souza

Co-Founder

Monisha joined Lester soon after Upper Crust began. Her foresight and skills brought a food revolution, lifting the brand to great heights. Together they opened a second branch of Upper Crust at Vijay Char Rasta and diversified into a fusion food chain. As their popularity soared, they introduced international cuisine to Ahmedabad — welcomed with open arms by the city.
